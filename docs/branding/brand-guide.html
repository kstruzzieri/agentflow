agentflow

Gate the flow. Prove the work.

A local protocol for accountable agent work. Plan steps flow through validation gates; each transition is recorded and provable. The mark is that idea — ledger rails passing through a gate, changing state from pending to verified.

plan lockedsteps ledgeredgate validatesproof recorded
01

The primitive

One shape, drawn from what the product does: ledger rails crossing a validation gate. It reuses the vertical bar in the wordmark, so mark and wordmark share a single element. No paper, no checkmark — that language belongs to proof documents, not to workflow.

Rails enter — pending

Gray inbound rails are plan.steps not yet validated. The ledger of work to do.

The gate — validate

The teal bar is the validation gate. Nothing advances without passing it. Same bar as the wordmark.

Rails exit — verified

Teal outbound rails + arrows: steps that passed and flow on as recorded proof.

Color system

Teal is the brand + gate + verified color. Mint is reserved for runtime PASS/success — never the general brand. Slate grounds keep the kit readable in light and dark contexts.
